-College Fees Payment Rules & Regulations

  1. Payment Deadline: All fees must be paid before the specified deadline. Late payments may attract penalties or restricted access to academic services.

  2. Accepted Payment Methods: Payments can be made via [Bank Transfer / Credit & Debit Cards / UPI / Net Banking] (mention available options). Cash payments are not accepted.

  3. Non-Refundable Fees: Once paid, tuition fees, examination fees, and other charges are non-refundable except under special circumstances approved by the administration.

  4. Payment Confirmation: Students must retain a copy of their payment receipt for future reference. Payment status updates may take up to 2 working days.

  5. Late Payment Penalty: A fine of 5% may be imposed for payments made after the due date. Continuous non-payment may lead to suspension from classes or exams.

  6. Transaction Failures: If a transaction fails but money is debited, students should wait [X] hours before retrying. If the issue persists, contact the finance office with proof of transaction.

  7. Fee Installments: If applicable, installment payments must be made on time as per the agreed schedule. Defaulting on any installment may lead to full fee demand immediately.

  8. Scholarships & Discounts: Students eligible for scholarships or discounts must ensure that the proper documents are submitted before the due date. Failure to do so may result in full fees being charged.

  9. No Third-Party Payments: Fees should be paid only through the student's registered portal. The college will not be responsible for payments made through unauthorized agents.

  10. Dispute Resolution: Any disputes regarding fee payments must be reported to the finance department within 3 days of the transaction.
